Work

    • Develop GPU-accelerated transport solvers that bridge kinetic and global MHD calculations on LANL and DOE supercomputers.
    • Coordinate multi-institutional efforts that connect numerical predictions with solar observations and laboratory experiments.
  • 2022.02 - 2024.09
    Research Scientist
    Department of Physics and Astronomy, Dartmouth College
    Coupled PIC, MHD, and transport simulations to interpret particle energization signatures in solar eruptions.

    • Mentored graduate and undergraduate students on reconnection-rate scaling, magnetotail reconnection, and simulation workflows.
  • 2020.02 - 2022.01
    Research Associate B
    Department of Physics and Astronomy, Dartmouth College
