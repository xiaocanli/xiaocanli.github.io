Using fully kinetic particle-in-cell simulations, I demonstrated that the
primary acceleration mechanism during magnetic reconnection is a Fermi-type
mechanism associated with particle curvature drift.
Find more details at [Li et al. 2015](/publication/2015-paper1) and
[Li et al. 2017](/publication/2017-paper1).
